AbAgIntPre


Descrtiption

The AbAgIntPre model is a powerful tool designed to predict antibody-antigen interactions by leveraging a combination of sequence-based encoding and convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture. It utilizes a unique encoding method known as composition of k-spaced amino acid pairs (CKSAAP) encoding to represent protein sequences. CKSAAP encoding captures the frequency of k-spaced amino acid pairs, allowing the model to consider short-range and long-range interactions critical for accurate prediction of protein function.
